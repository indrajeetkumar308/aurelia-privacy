Privacy Policy

Hotel Aurelia Pearl, operated by Rising Colors Hospitality Private Limited, Patna, Bihar, India.
Effective date: 11 July 2026 · Last updated: 11 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect when you use our website, booking engine, WhatsApp assistant, or stay with us, how we use it, who we share it with, and the rights you have over it. By using our services you agree to this policy.

1. Who we are

The data controller is Rising Colors Hospitality Private Limited ("we", "us"), operating Hotel Aurelia Pearl. For any privacy request, contact us at the details in Section 10.

2. Information we collect

  • Identity & contact: your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Booking details: stay dates, room type, number of guests, preferences, and special requests.
  • Check-in / KYC: where legally required for hotel check-in, a government ID type and number, address, date of birth, nationality. ID numbers are stored encrypted.
  • Payment information: payments are processed by our payment gateway (Razorpay). We do not store your full card, UPI, or bank details on our servers.
  • Account & login: if you sign in, we receive your name and email from the login method you choose (phone OTP, email, Google, or Facebook).
  • Usage & device: IP address, browser/device information, and cookies or analytics identifiers (only where you have consented).
  • Communications: messages you send us over WhatsApp, Instagram, email, or our website chat.

3. How we use your information

  • To take and manage your bookings, check-in, and stay.
  • To process payments and issue confirmations and invoices.
  • To communicate with you about your booking (via WhatsApp and email).
  • To comply with legal, tax, and hospitality record-keeping obligations.
  • To improve our website and services, and — only with your consent — to send offers and marketing.

4. Who we share it with

We share personal data only with service providers that help us operate, and only the minimum needed:

  • Razorpay — to process payments.
  • Meta (WhatsApp & Instagram) — to send and receive booking messages.
  • Google / Facebook — if you choose to log in with them (used only to verify your sign-in).
  • Analytics (Google Analytics, Meta Pixel) — only where you have accepted analytics cookies.

We do not sell your personal data.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies to run the site, and analytics/marketing cookies only after you consent via our cookie banner. You can decline non-essential cookies at any time.

6. Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed for the purposes above and to meet legal record-keeping requirements, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

7. Security

We protect your data with encryption in transit (HTTPS), encryption of sensitive fields such as ID numbers at rest, hashed passwords, access controls, and other safeguards.

8. Your rights

You may request to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your account and personal data ("right to erasure") — booking records may be retained in de-identified form as required by law.
  • Withdraw marketing consent at any time.

To exercise any of these, contact us using Section 10.

9. Children

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect data from children without a parent or guardian.
